AirHelp

AirHelp is an iOS and Android application that lets you check if your flight is eligible for compensation and guides you through the whole process of complaints. As of March 2017, AirHelp has assisted more than 2 million passengers in 35 countries all over Europe and the USA. We cooperated with the company in 2015 and we rewrote available then iOS application using native technology and new design.

main features:

• Push notifications

• Facebook and Google integration

• dynamic survey
