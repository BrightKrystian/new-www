  • Web solutions (AngularJS, ReactJS, Socket.io, Leaflet)
  • Backend solutions (Java/Kotlin, Node.js, Express, Ruby-on-Rails, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Docker, AWS)
  • Continous delivery: git, TeamCity, fastlane, gradle
